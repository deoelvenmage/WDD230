Target Audience

The target audience is current and prospective business owners in the Ricksburg area.

Example Personas

Persona 1

Name: Bob Rommund

Demographics: Middle-aged male, married, has Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering

Occupation: Manager and lead repairman at a local business for home appliance repair and maintenance.

Goals: Wants to expand the business's reach and reputation. Often spends work taking calls and going out doing repair work himself.

Environment: Since he's often on the move going from one home to another, he doesn't have a sit-down computer he works at often, and relies on his smartphone for just about all of his electronic contact.

Persona 2

Name: Shayla Wheeler

Demographics: Young woman, unmarried, Associate's Degree in Accounting

Occupation: Chef and restaurant manager for a family-owned restaurant in a nearby town. What she does at work often varies depending on what is needed, and she also is concerned with meeting health standards.

Goals: Would like to establish a new branch of her family's restaurant. Works as a manager and a chef in the original restaurant in another town, but is looking to move to Ricksburg to expand the family business.

Environment: She has a smartphone and a laptop she uses equally when out and about; she would describe herself as very comfortable using the internet and computers.

Scenarios

  • Where do I go/who do I contact to begin acquiring a business license?
  • How can I get in touch with other restaurant owners about suppliers?
  • What are the costs and fees associated with running a business in these parts?
  • I want to expand the lot for my business; how do I go about that?
  • Are there any kinds of financial protections or tax stimuli for local business owners?
